Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

Skill to Income does not require user registration or personal information to access educational content. Anonymous usage data is collected through Vercel Analytics to improve platform performance.

2. Email Subscriptions

When voluntarily subscribing to updates, we collect email addresses solely for sending course announcements and platform news. Subscribers may unsubscribe at any time.

3. Data Storage

Progress tracking data is stored locally in your browser. No personal information is stored on our servers. Email subscriptions are securely stored in encrypted databases.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize Vercel for hosting and Supabase for database management. YouTube embeds may collect viewing data according to their policies.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete any personal information you have provided. Contact us for assistance.

6. Contact Information

For privacy concerns or data requests, please contact our support team through the contact page.
