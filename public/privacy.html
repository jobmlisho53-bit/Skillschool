1. Introduction

Welcome to Income School ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free educational platform.

Important: Income School does NOT require user accounts, logins, or registration. You can access all courses anonymously.

2. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information because:

  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No email collection
  • No payment processing
  • No cookies for tracking (except essential Vercel analytics)

Automatically Collected Information (Anonymous)

Our hosting platform (Vercel) may collect anonymous usage data:

  • Page views (which courses are visited)
  • Approximate geographic region (country level only)
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • Browser type

This data is fully anonymous and cannot identify individual users.

3. How We Use Information

Anonymous usage data helps us:

  • Understand which courses are most popular
  • Improve our content and platform
  • Monitor platform performance

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel - Hosting and anonymous analytics
  • Supabase - Database (stores course content only, no user data)
  • YouTube - Video content (embedded YouTube players)

These services have their own privacy policies. YouTube may collect viewing data according to their terms.

5. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Vercel may use essential cookies for platform functionality. YouTube embeds may set cookies according to YouTube's policies.

6. Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained by Vercel according to their data retention policy. No personal data is stored.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Since we collect no personal data, there is no personal data to access, delete, or export. If you have concerns, please contact us.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for general audience. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
