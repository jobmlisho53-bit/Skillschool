Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Skill to Income collects minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Anonymous Usage Data: Page views, course progress, and lesson completion (stored locally or anonymously)
  • Email Address: Only if you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter
  • Feedback: Voluntary course ratings and suggestions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Track your course progress (stored locally in your browser)
  • Improve our courses based on anonymous feedback
  • Send newsletter updates (only if you subscribed)

3. Data Storage

Your progress is stored locally in your browser. We do not require accounts or store personal information on our servers. Email addresses for newsletters are stored securely in our database.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database for course content and anonymous feedback
  • Vercel: Hosting and anonymous analytics
  • YouTube: Video content (embedded players)

5. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Essential cookies may be used for platform functionality.

6. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal information (except email for newsletter), there is no personal data to access or delete. You can unsubscribe from newsletters at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for general audience. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
